Where is Morocco ?

Morocco is at the top of the ranking of the world's gastronomic destinations.
The richness of Moroccan gastronomy has earned Morocco the second place, ahead of Italy and France.
Moroccan cuisine is fueled by interactions and exchanges with many cultures and nations over the centuries.
Moroccan cuisine is usually a mix of Arab, Berber, Andalusi, and Mediterranean cuisines,
with minimal European (French and Spanish) and sub-Saharan influences. Like the rest of the Maghrebi cuisine,
Moroccan cuisine has more in common with Middle Eastern cuisine than with the rest of Africa.
